About Us

Eagle Engineering Works India Private Limited is a 1994 established manufacturing company based in Mumbai (Maharashtra, India). Also reputed as a trusted importer and exporter, we are rapidly growing in the industry and already enjoys the support from a large clientele. We offer a wide range of superior quality Eagle Swivel Plain-Break Castors, Delux Single Glass Runners, Derling Bush Bearing Six Fix Wheel Castors, Roller Fix Castor Wheels, Wall Mounted Self Flat Brackets,a nd other products at fair prices. Connecting with us proves to be beneficial for our customers!

Our Vision

At Eagle Engineering Works India Private Limited, our vision is to lead in customer satisfaction and champion the "Make in India" initiative on a global scale. We strive to create products that not only meet but exceed the highest standards of utility, aesthetics, and sustainability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop and deliver premium-quality products that surpass the expectations of our customers. We aim to achieve this by continuously innovating and adhering to the best practices in manufacturing and design. By focusing on the needs and desires of our customers, we ensure that every product we create is a testament to our dedication to excellence.

Our Quality

Quality is at the heart of Eagle Engineering Works India. We source the finest materials and employ rigorous quality control processes to ensure that every product meets our high standards. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to maintain the superior quality that our customers have come to expect.
