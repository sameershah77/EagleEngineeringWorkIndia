Welcome to Eagle Engineering Works India For over 30 years, Eagle Engineering Works India has been a leader in the manufacturing of high-quality furniture fittings and hardware items, including Casters and kitchen appliances. Our long-standing dedication to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has established us as a trusted name in the industry. From our humble beginnings to our current state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, we have remained committed to delivering superior products that meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1994, began as a small workshop focused on creating exceptional other furniture fittings items. Over the years, our commitment to craftsmanship and quality has fueled our growth and expansion. Today, we operate with advanced manufacturing technologies and a skilled workforce that embodies our founding principles of precision, reliability, and excellence.

Our Quality

Quality is at the heart of Eagle Engineering Works India. We source the finest materials and employ rigorous quality control processes to ensure that every product meets our high standards. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to maintain the superior quality that our customers have come to expect.

Our commitment to sustainability is evident in our use of eco-friendly materials and manufacturing practices that minimize our environmental impact. We strive to create products that not only perform exceptionally but also contribute to a greener future.
